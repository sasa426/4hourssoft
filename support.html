App Support

Need help with our apps? Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our team.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues, please email us with details about the problem, your device model, and iOS version.

How do I request a refund?

For iOS apps, refunds are handled directly by Apple. Please visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

I have a feature request.

We love hearing from our users! Feel free to send us your suggestions via email.

Where can I see the Privacy Policy?

You can view our general privacy policy here, or check the specific policy for each app in the app's settings.
