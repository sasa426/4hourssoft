Introduction

4hours Soft ("we," "our," or "us") operates the "Image to PDF : Fast & Simple" application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Privacy First Design

Our app does not require account registration and collects minimal data to ensure your privacy.

2

Information We Collect

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We collect very limited information:

  • Photo Library Access: The app requires photo library permission to select images for PDF conversion. Images are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device type, operating system version) for app functionality and crash reporting purposes.
We Do NOT Collect

Personal information (name, email, phone number), location data, contacts or address book, your photos or files (they stay on your device).

3

How We Use Information

The limited information we access is used only for:

  • Converting images to PDF format on your device
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
4

Data Storage

All converted PDF files and app data are stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on external servers. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed.

5

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics or advertising. These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties.

6

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since our app does not require registration or collect personal data, it is safe for users of all ages.

7

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
